Flying overseas means one dead phone can ruin maps, payments, and hotel check-in. These are the criteria we use before adding anything to **Outsea Picks**.

## What to look for

- **Capacity**: 10,000–20,000 mAh is the sweet spot for carry-on rules and daily use.
- **Ports**: USB-C PD in + out; a second USB-A port is still handy for older gear.
- **Weight**: Under 250 g feels pocketable; heavier packs are fine for backpacks only.

::: tip
Check your airline's watt-hour (Wh) limit before buying. Most brands print Wh on the label.
:::
