**Last updated:** June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Outsea Picks** (“we”, “us”, “our”), operated at **idove.dpdns.org**, collects and uses information when you visit our website.

## Who we are

Outsea Picks is a product recommendation and affiliate content site for global shoppers. We publish guides, comparisons, and curated picks. Some links on this site are affiliate links.

## Information we collect

When you visit our site, we and our service providers may automatically collect:

- **Technical data:** IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring URL, pages viewed, and approximate location derived from IP
- **Usage data:** Date and time of visit, time spent on pages, and click patterns on internal links
- **Cookies and similar technologies:** Small files stored on your device to remember preferences and measure traffic

We do **not** knowingly collect sensitive personal data such as payment card numbers, government IDs, or account passwords through this site. Purchases made via third-party retailers (including Amazon) are handled on their platforms under their privacy policies.

## How we use information

We use collected information to:

- Operate, maintain, and improve the website
- Understand which content is useful to visitors
- Measure traffic and site performance
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

## Affiliate links and Amazon Associates

Some pages contain **affiliate links**, including links to **Amazon.com**, **Amazon.co.jp**, and other Amazon marketplaces. If you click an affiliate link and make a qualifying purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

Amazon and other partners may use **cookies or similar tracking** to attribute sales to our site. These third parties collect information according to their own policies. For Amazon’s practices, see [Amazon.com Privacy Notice](https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=468496).

**Affiliate disclosure:** As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases.

## Third-party services

Our site may use or be served through:

- **Cloudflare** — hosting, CDN, security, and analytics (see [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/))
- **Amazon Associates** — affiliate tracking when you click product links

We do not control how these providers process data. We encourage you to review their policies.

## Cookies

We may use cookies for:

- Basic site functionality and language preferences
- Traffic measurement and performance analysis
- Affiliate attribution through partner networks

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site features. Clearing cookies may prevent affiliate partners from attributing a purchase to our link.

## Data retention

Server logs and analytics data are retained only as long as needed for operations, security, and reporting, typically for a limited period defined by our hosting and analytics providers.

## Your choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Because we collect limited personal data directly, most requests can be addressed by:

- Adjusting cookie settings in your browser
- Using private browsing modes
- Contacting us using the email below

## Children’s privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## International visitors

Our site is available globally. If you access it from outside your home country, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
